Frequently Asked Questions

What are cloud implementation services? +
In today's timeline, cloud implementation is a must for versatility, dexterity, and rapid deployment. It has become a necessary imperative for businesses to maintain an edge.
Why is Cloud implementation necessary for your services? +
Cloud implementation allows businesses to demand development and delivery of their services at an unprecedented rate. Moving to the cloud offers critical benefits like rapid deployment and on-demand technology.
What is Cloud Migration? +
Cloud Migration is the process of moving applications and data from on-premise data centers to a more secure and scalable environment, or moving workloads from one cloud provider to another.
What are some prerequisites and necessary steps for cloud implementation? +
Our approach involves a four-step process: Assessment (understanding business scenarios and technical feasibility), Planning (architecture design and roadmap), Deployment (execution and transition), and Optimization.
What contingency plans do we offer? +
We provide Disaster Recovery and Management services, which include application security testing, management of all backups, and customized disaster recovery plans to ensure business continuity.
