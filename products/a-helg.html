Features

What is A-Helg?

A-Helg monitors the entire infrastructure stack. From operating systems to application runtimes, it provides real-time tracking of CPU, Memory, I/O throughput, and resource usage. Data and model drift detection ensures your AI systems remain accurate over time.

Comprehensive Insights

A-Helg delivers end-to-end multimodal perception and reasoning. It combines object detection, tracking, scene understanding, and LLM-driven decisioning into a unified pipeline. It supports flexible deployments (cloud, on-prem, edge) to optimize latency, cost, and accuracy.

Customisable Graphs

Interactive visualizations for metrics, detections, and events. Choose chart types like line, bar, heatmap, scatter, and object-overlap views. Fully configurable metrics, time windows, and interactive controls (filters, zoom, drill-down) give you full control over your data.
